Symptom
All 11 items show status "Picked" on the cooler screen after completing the COOLER-ROUTE-412 batch. Both boxes are open. Clicking "Assign" on any item appears to do nothing — boxes remain empty (no items count, no first/last stop).

Step 2 — Diagnose the root cause in shell
pythonfrom app import app, db
from sqlalchemy import text

with app.app_context():
    # Check what the cooler queue rows look like for this route
    rows = db.session.execute(text("""
        SELECT bpq.id, bpq.invoice_no, bpq.item_code, 
               bpq.status, bpq.pick_zone_type,
               i.route_id, s.delivery_date,
               cb.route_id as box_route_id,
               cb.delivery_date as box_delivery_date
        FROM batch_pick_queue bpq
        JOIN invoices i ON i.invoice_no = bpq.invoice_no
        LEFT JOIN shipments s ON s.id = i.route_id
        LEFT JOIN cooler_boxes cb ON cb.route_id = i.route_id
        WHERE bpq.pick_zone_type = 'cooler'
        AND bpq.status = 'picked'
        LIMIT 5
    """)).fetchall()
    
    for r in rows:
        print(dict(r))
This shows whether the invoice route_id, shipment delivery_date, and box route_id/delivery_date all match. If any mismatch exists, the cross-route check in queue_assign_box rejects the assignment.

However, the Items count ("—") should update immediately when items are assigned. Check whether cooler_box_items rows are being created. After a successful Assign click, verify:
