2) Key design decision: integrate returns into discrepancies or keep separate?
Recommendation (best practice)

Keep route_return_handover as invoice-level custody chain, and use delivery_discrepancies for line-level issues + warehouse verification.

Why:

A failed invoice return is a physical handover event (even if no specific SKU discrepancy was recorded).

A discrepancy is per item/line and may happen even when invoice is delivered.

So: don’t overload delivery_discrepancies to become the handover ledger.
Instead, extend discrepancies to support warehouse verification + credit note workflow.
