Shipped	Order left warehouse, in transit	On Shipment
Delivered	Customer received the order	Archive
Delivery Failed	Courier could not deliver	On Shipment
Returned to Warehouse	Returned due to failed delivery or customer return	Open Orders
Cancelled	Order cancelled after shipping or return	Archive
