#### Required Action

1. Identify every occurrence of `_role_ok()` in `routes_communications`, `blueprints/communications.py`, `blueprints/sms.py`, and any related modules.
2. Migrate each call to use `has_permission(current_user, 'comms.<specific_key>')` from `services.permissions`.
3. Map hardcoded role checks to specific permission keys. Suggested mapping:

   | Old check | New permission key |
   |-----------|-------------------|
   | `role in ('admin', 'crm_admin')` for sending SMS | `comms.send_sms` |

   | `role in ('admin', 'crm_admin')` for viewing logs | `comms.view_logs` |
