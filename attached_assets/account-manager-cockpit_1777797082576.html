How this page maps to today's data Header info pulls from the customer + delivery-slot tables. Hero KPIs and trend chart use dw_invoice_lines.gross_profit / gross_margin_pct (already populated). Active offers and offer status come from crm_customer_offer_current (margin_status, line_status, sold_qty_4w). White space & cross-sell rules from the analytics-jobs output. The "Offers he could get" panel is a new query: items with positive 90d revenue × no row in crm_customer_offer_current, joined against rule eligibility. Recommended actions are ranked by Claude (Anthropic API) combining all of the above with a simple GP-impact estimator. Greek output with English trade terminology preserved.
