Price Index vs Market — Help

Why does Pricing Analytics revenue sometimes look lower than Customer 360?
Pricing Analytics benchmarks only the Top N items (default Top 50) for performance and accuracy. It also uses positive sales only by default (returns/credit notes are excluded unless you tick Include credits).

So the “Revenue” shown in Price Index is not your total customer sales — it is the revenue covered by the benchmarked Top N items.

Use Coverage % to see what share of the customer’s gross positive sales is included in the benchmark.
