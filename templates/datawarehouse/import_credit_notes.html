Why this page exists: The PS365 loyalty endpoint (list_loyalty_invoices_header) does not return accounting-side credit notes. Import them here so that pbi_fact_sales correctly nets them out of net_sales on the KPI dashboard. Each credit note appears in the view as a CREDIT NOTE row with a negative line_total_excl. Enter/upload amounts as positive numbers — the view negates them automatically.
