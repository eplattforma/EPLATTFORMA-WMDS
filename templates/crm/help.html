CRM Dashboard

The CRM Dashboard gives you a complete view of your customer base with real-time metrics and detailed customer information.

Key Metrics (KPIs)

Orders KPI

Total number of orders in the system

Total Customers

Active customers meeting current filter criteria

Action Required

Customers needing follow-up or intervention

Done

Completed customer interactions

Customer Table

The main table displays customer information including:

  • Customer Code & Name: Unique identifier and company name
  • Classification: Customer type (Customer, EKO, Petrolina, SHELL, Monitor, At Risk, Frozen)
  • District: Geographic region
  • Last Login: Days since last platform login (shown as colored pill)
  • Orders: Number of open orders
  • Cart: Abandoned cart status and amount
  • Actions: Quick action buttons for customer interaction

Expanding Customer Details

Click on a customer row to expand and view:

  • District - Geographic location
  • Area - Sales area assignment
  • Postal Code - Delivery postal code
  • Town - City/municipality
  • Delivery Slot - Next delivery day
  • PS Code - Primary system customer code
  • Last Login - Timestamp of last login
  • Last Invoice - Date of most recent invoice
Tip: Expand customer rows to see complete address and communication history details.

Search & Filtering

Use the filter bar to quickly find customers matching your criteria. Filters can be combined for precise searching.

Quick Search

Search by customer name or code in the search box at the top of the filter bar. Results appear as you type.

Advanced Filters

Classification

Filter by customer classification type:

Customer EKO Petrolina SHELL Monitor At Risk Frozen

Select multiple classifications by clicking the Classification dropdown.

District

Filter customers by geographic region. Multi-select available.

Click "District" dropdown to choose one or more districts.

Login ≤ d (Days)

Show customers who logged in within the last N days:

  • 0 = Logged in today
  • 1 = Logged in within 1 day
  • 7 = Logged in within 1 week
  • 30 = Logged in within 1 month
Checkboxes

Quick toggles for common filters:

  • Assisted: Customers using assisted ordering
  • Expected: Expected to order this cycle
  • Cart: Customers with abandoned carts

Apply & Reset

Click Apply to update the customer list. Click the reset icon to clear all filters and start fresh.

Pro Tip: Combine filters (e.g., Classification="At Risk" + Login≤7 days) to find high-priority customers needing attention.

Review Ordering

The Review Ordering page helps you manage order delivery windows and track customer ordering status.

Open Delivery Windows

At the top of the page, you'll see all currently open delivery windows with countdown timers. Green indicates active windows; red indicates windows closing within 4 hours.

Info: An open delivery window means customers can place orders for that delivery date.

Review KPIs

  • Follow Up: Customers who need to be contacted
  • Waiting: Customers waiting for delivery
  • Ordered: Customers who have ordered
  • Ordered+Cart: Customers with both orders and abandoned carts
  • Done: Review completed
  • Cart: Customers with abandoned carts

Understanding Follow-Up State Logic

The "Follow Up" state is automatically assigned when a customer meets ANY of these criteria:

Follow-Up Triggers
  1. Manual Follow-Up Flag: You manually marked this customer as needing follow-up
  2. Assisted Ordering (No Order): Customer uses assisted ordering service BUT hasn't placed an order yet
  3. Abandoned Cart (No Order): Customer has items in cart BUT hasn't submitted an order
  4. Logged In During Window (No Order): Customer logged in during the delivery window BUT didn't place an order
  5. Expected This Cycle (No Order): You marked them as "Expected" to order BUT they haven't
Key Insight: Follow-up state means "Action Required" — this customer shows buying intent (cart, login, assisted request, or expectation) but hasn't completed their order. Your job is to follow up and help them complete it.
Strategy Tip: Prioritize "Follow Up" customers in your ordering review. They're most likely to convert with a gentle reminder or assistance.

Update Customer State

Click on a customer to open the detail drawer. Options include:

  • Change State: Update review status (Follow Up → Waiting → Ordered → Done)
  • Mark Assisted: Flag for assisted ordering
  • Add Note: Record interaction notes
  • Expected This Cycle: Mark if expected to order
  • Select for Bulk Communication: Use checkbox to bulk message

Bulk Communications

Select multiple customers using checkboxes and click "Send MicroSMS" to message them at once.

Warning: Bulk messages are sent immediately. Review selections before sending.

Communications Hub

The Communications Hub allows you to reach customers through multiple channels for targeted engagement.

Supported Channels

MicroSMS

Send SMS text messages to customer phone numbers

Voice Calls

Initiate automated voice calls for urgent messages

WhatsApp

Send WhatsApp messages for rich communication

Push Notifications

Send app push notifications via OneSignal

Send a Message

  1. Click Communications in the main navigation
  2. Select a customer or group
  3. Choose your preferred channel (SMS, WhatsApp, etc.)
  4. Write your message using available templates or custom text
  5. Click Send to deliver the message

Communication Log

Every message sent is logged with:

  • Channel used
  • Timestamp
  • Delivery status (Sent, Failed, Pending)
  • Message content
  • Sent by (user name)
Tip: View communication history by expanding a customer row on the dashboard to see all previous interactions.

Price Offers

View and manage special pricing offers available to customers based on order history and business rules.

Understanding Price Offers

Price offers are discount rules automatically applied based on:

  • Customer classification
  • Order volume
  • Loyalty history
  • Seasonal promotions
  • Bulk purchase quantities

View Customer Price Offers

  1. Open a customer's detail view from the dashboard
  2. Click the Price Offers tab
  3. Review available offers with discount percentages
  4. Sort by discount, SKU, or rule name
  5. Search for specific products

Sorting Options

  • By Discount %: Highest discounts first
  • By Rule: Offer rule name
  • By Product: Product SKU or name
Tip: Share high-discount offers with customers to encourage ordering during review periods.

  • Start each day by checking the "Action Required" KPI count
  • Use the "At Risk" classification filter to monitor at-risk customers

  • Check delivery status after sending bulk messages

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is a customer not showing in my filtered results?

A: Check that the customer's classification is enabled for the current view, has a delivery slot assigned, and meets all active filter criteria. Click the reset button to clear filters and verify the customer exists.

Q: How are delivery windows determined?

A: Delivery windows are based on customer delivery slot assignments and the order submission deadline set in system settings. Check the window countdown timer at the top of Review Ordering for exact close times.

Q: What does "Login ≤ 0" mean?

A: This shows customers who logged in today. Login ≤ 7 shows anyone who logged in within the past 7 days, Login ≤ 30 shows the past month, etc.

Q: Can I undo a state change?

A: Yes. Open the customer drawer and select a different state. The system tracks all state changes in the customer history.

Q: How do I know if a message was delivered?

A: Check the communication log by expanding a customer row. Each message shows its delivery status (Sent, Failed, Pending, or Delivered).

Q: What's the difference between "Cart" and "Ordered"?

A: "Cart" indicates an abandoned shopping session (customer started but didn't complete an order). "Ordered" means they submitted a completed order for delivery.

Q: Can I bulk edit multiple customers at once?

A: You can send bulk communications to multiple selected customers. For state changes, you need to update each customer individually through their detail view.

Q: How often is customer data updated?

A: Customer login data, order information, and cart status are updated in near real-time as actions occur in the system.
