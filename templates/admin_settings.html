Picking Process Settings
When enabled, pickers will see a confirmation window with product image before confirming each pick. When disabled, picks will be automatically confirmed without showing the confirmation window.
When enabled, product images will be displayed above the item name on the picking screen. When disabled, no images will be shown during picking, which may improve loading times.
When enabled, pickers must provide a reason when skipping items. When disabled, items can be skipped without providing a reason.
Enter the available skip reasons, one per line. These will appear in the dropdown when skipping items. Always include "Other" as an option.
Enter the available exception reasons, one per line. These will appear in the dropdown when reporting exceptions during picking. Always include "Other" as an option.
When enabled, pickers will see a prominent warning badge "⚠️ PICK X UNITS ⚠️" whenever the quantity to pick is not equal to 1. This helps prevent mistakes when picking multiple units.
